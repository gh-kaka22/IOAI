About the Competition

Discover the mission, vision, and impact of the International Olympiad in Artificial Intelligence

The International Olympiad in Artificial Intelligence (IOAI) is a prestigious global competition that challenges and inspires the next generation of AI innovators.

Designed for exceptional young minds, the IOAI provides a unique platform to showcase talent, solve real-world problems, and connect with peers from around the globe.

Key Objectives

  • Promote AI education and research among pre-university students worldwide
  • Identify and nurture exceptional talent in artificial intelligence
  • Foster international collaboration and cultural exchange
  • Explore ethical implications and responsible development of AI

Competition Focus Areas

Machine Learning

Algorithms, neural networks, and predictive modeling challenges

Robotics

Autonomous systems and intelligent control challenges

NLP

Language processing, generation, and understanding tasks

Computer Vision

Image recognition, processing, and analysis problems
